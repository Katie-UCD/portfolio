EBS Social Media
Design System

A motion identity built from letterform geometry. 50+ templates that gave EBS a distinct digital presence and tripled content production speed.

Role Solo Designer
Timeline 2020–Present
Tools After Effects · Illustrator · Photoshop
EBS transition video. The motion system in action. After Effects
Overview

EBS had a new brand identity. But nobody had defined how it should move.

When EBS refreshed their brand, the new guidelines were built entirely around out-of-home advertising. There was no digital visual language. No templates, no motion principles, no defined way for the brand to show up on social media.

The marketing team was producing content but it looked inconsistent. Different people making different things with no shared system behind them. Each post took around two hours to produce and there was no way to scale.

My job was to create that digital language from scratch; to take the EBS letterforms, colour palette and photography direction and build a motion system that could live on Instagram, be used by a team of non-specialists, and still look like one coherent brand.

My Role

End-to-end design system ownership. Visual direction, motion design, template production, and handoff to an external agency and in-house marketing team.

Built For

A mix of in-house designers and external social media agencies producing content day-to-day without always having a designer available.

Skills Used

Design Systems Motion Design After Effects Brand Adaptation Illustrator Template Production
Problem

A brand with no digital voice.

The existing brand guidelines were designed for print and out-of-home. There were no rules for how EBS should look or move on social media. Every post was being made from scratch, inconsistently, by different people.

Approach

Build the system, not just the assets.

Rather than designing individual posts, I created a reusable motion system rooted in the EBS letterforms and brand geometry. The goal was templates anyone could use; design decisions already made, brand already locked in.

Outcome

3x faster. Two years of awards.

Production time dropped from two hours per post to under 30 minutes. The system produced 200+ posts in its first six months. EBS won Best Instagram Brand at the Social Media Awards in 2021 and 2022.

Static social post. The curved line motif and photography direction applied across the system. Illustrator · Photoshop
The System

Every element was designed to be reusable, not just repeatable.

The core visual idea came from the EBS letterforms themselves. The curves, the geometry, the weight of the letters, these became the raw material for the motion system. Circles, arcs, and sweeping lines were abstracted from the logo and turned into a flexible set of motion elements.

The photography direction was defined separately, giving the team clear rules on image style, cropping, and how to combine photography with the graphic layer.
